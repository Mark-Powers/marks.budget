Here is how I use this site

  1. Expenses go in Ledger. Income also goes in the ledger as negative
  2. The category I use for big categories: bills, groceries, restaurants
  3. Inside tags (which aren't csv yet) I have specifics: internet, coffee, gas
  4. Summary gives some view of where money is going and coming from
  5. Expected is what I expect in an out, for a period of days. For example, I pay internet every 31 days, and renew my domain every 365 days
  6. Goals can be used to subdivide net savings into categories. It is still W.I.P.
